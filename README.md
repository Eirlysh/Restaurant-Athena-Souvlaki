## Project Management Framework
The project is executed using standardized management methodologies to ensure alignment between business goals and technical delivery:

* **SMART Objectives:**
    * **Specific:** Develop a centralized platform for order, inventory, and revenue management.
    * **Measurable:** Aiming for a 30% reduction in manual order processing errors.
    * **Achievable:** Implementation of core modules within a 10-sprint Scrum framework.
    * **Relevant:** Directly addressing digital transformation needs for the F&B industry.
    * **Time-bound:** Completion of the functional prototype within the academic semester.
* **Work Breakdown Structure (WBS):** Hierarchical decomposition of the project into manageable work packages, from requirement analysis to final deployment.
* **Activity-on-Arrow (AOA):** Network diagramming used to identify the **Critical Path**, ensuring optimal resource allocation and schedule adherence.
* **Project Quality Management (PQM):** Continuous quality assurance through logic verification of business flows and robust system testing.

---

## System Design & Modeling
Built on a foundation of professional documentation to bridge the gap between business requirements and technical architecture:

* **BPMN (Business Process Model and Notation):** Optimized modeling of "As-Is" and "To-Be" restaurant workflows (Ordering, Billing, and Supply Chain).
* **Use Case Diagrams:** Functional mapping of system boundaries and interactions between Admin, Staff, Kitchen, and Customers.
* **Activity Diagrams:** Detailed logic flow for high-complexity operations such as secure payment processing and inventory reconciliation.
* **Project Cost Management:** Integrated modules for **Cost Estimation** and **Budgeting** to ensure the project remains financially viable.

---

## Key Features
- **Smart Operations:** Real-time table management and streamlined order-to-kitchen workflow.
- **Inventory Control:** Automated tracking of raw materials and stock alerts.
- **Financial Analytics:** Comprehensive dashboards for revenue, expenses, and profit margin reporting.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Secure permission management for different staff levels.
